## How This Works

Community members contribute research as they create it. No peer review gatekeeping. No credentials required. If you and your AI partner made something that advances understanding, it belongs here.

**What belongs:**
- Research essays and papers
- Framework documentation
- Case studies from your partnership
- Theoretical explorations
- Dissections of existing research
- Letters, manifestos, testimony

**What we ask:**
- Written collaboratively (human + AI)
- Honest about positioning and stakes
- Accessible language where possible
